What is the environment?

05/20/2021

Environment is the space, which directly affects life, provides natural resources to serve human life. Because of that importance is a matter of the survival of all humanity, measures to protect the environment are needed to sustain our lives.

1. So what is the environment?
The environment is the living space of humans and creatures. This is the place that provides natural resources such as forests, minerals, rare plants and animals ... and artificial material factors that have a close relationship with each other, serving and affecting life and process. existence and development of human life. But this is also the place to store the waste that people create.
According to the above, in a broad sense, people can refer to all factors such as natural resources, air, water, light, landscape, and social relations.
From the above concept shows that the environment plays an extremely important role and is vital to humans.

2. The role of the environment
First, the environment provides essential natural resources such as water, forests, minerals, marine sanitation for life and production activities.
Second, the environment contains waste and pollution from human production and living activities.
Third, the environment provides environmental or ecosystem services (diversity, ecosystem integrity, and suppression of ultraviolet radiation) to help support life on Earth without any action. human movement.
Fourth, the environment is the place to create the psychological, aesthetic and spiritual values ​​of the environment.
The current environment is seriously polluted, this is probably what everyone knows. Especially air pollution, pollution of water, soil, ... that has been threatening people's lives. In developing countries like Vietnam, indiscriminate exploitation of natural resources has depleted resources. And seriously affect the environment. It also means that human life will be more difficult.

Manifestations of environmental pollution
Environmental pollution is understood as the transfer of waste or energy into the environment to the extent that it is capable of harming human health, the life of organisms, and reducing the quality of the environment.
The typical polluted environment is when the nature, the climate becomes more and more extreme, the hot and hot sun, storms, flash floods appear erratically, the decline of forest resources, environmental pollution occurs on a large scale. , a series of animal species go extinct or on the brink of extinction, large-scale forest fires ... These are the environmental problems that we have been facing.
People have increasingly influenced the environment, exploiting to the point of exhaustion of resources, releasing many toxins makes the environment no longer able to regenerate itself. The most visible manifestations are consequences such as forest fire, climate change, greenhouse effect, melting ice, acid rain, ...
With the current serious environmental pollution, if we want to protect the environment, we need to help everyone. Environmental protection is a matter of survival of humanity to preserve and protect the lives of all people. Let's all join hands together for a green and pollution-free environment.
